ABOUT MYSELF

I am a researcher and project manager in the Text Analytics (LT) group at DFKI Berlin and focus on natural language processing and machine learning with a high interest in the biomedical and clinical domain. Currently I am working within the research projects MACSS ( Medical Allround-Care Service Solutions) and BigMedilytics (Big Data for Medical Analytics).
